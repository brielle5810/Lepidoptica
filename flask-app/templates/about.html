About Lepidoptica

This is a tool to assist in digitization efforts for the Florida Museum of Natural History McGuire Center by extracting and parse metadata from butterfly specimen labels. This metadata is to be uploaded to the FLMNH's Specify Lepidoptera Collection database, available online here, for researchers and museums.

Users can upload batches of images and our website will attempt to transcribe their text content using machine learning technology, specifically a fine-tuned model using EasyOCR. Next, users can edit the transcriptions from the web interface and save them as a .csv file for bulk uploading to the collection database.

Designed by students Alexia Rangel Krashenitsa, Alex Harvey, Brielle Stokes, and Katja Karoleski at the University of Florida for Senior Project Spring 2025.

Supervised by curators Keith Willmott and Vaughn Shirey.

Special thanks to the Florida Museum of Natural History and the McGuire Center for Lepidoptera and Biodiversity.
